**Illusory Progress – Day 33: The Cashless Society** 💳

**Short Recap of the Series**  
Day 2: Leasing  
Day 3: Heat pumps  
Day 4: Electric cars  
Day 5: The 15-minute city  
Day 6: Everything as a subscription  

**The Narrative**  
Cash is dirty, outdated and mainly used by criminals.  
A cashless society is safer, easier, more efficient and better for the climate. Everyone pays with card or phone. Who still wants to carry coins and notes?

**The Reality – Hard Truth**

**Layer 1: Total Loss of Privacy**  
Every payment is permanently recorded. Where you are, what you buy, when, how often and with whom. Your entire economic life becomes one big traceable profile.

**Layer 2: Complete Dependence**  
No cash = fully dependent on banks, internet, electricity and systems.  
One outage, one blocked account, or one “technical issue” and you literally can’t buy anything.

**Layer 3: Programmable Money**  
In a fully digital world, money can be programmed:  
- Money that expires after a certain date  
- Money that can only be spent on specific products  
- Money that doesn’t work outside your own district  

This is not conspiracy theory. It is being actively tested worldwide with CBDCs (Central Bank Digital Currencies).

**Layer 4: Social and Behavioral Control**  
Bought too much meat? Drove too often? Too low a CO₂ score? Your payment options can be restricted or adjusted. This is Social Credit with a smile.

**Layer 5: Shift of Power**  
You lose control. Banks and governments gain total control over who is still allowed to participate in the economy.

**The OIM Way Out**  
- Pay with cash as much as possible.  
- Always keep an emergency cash reserve.  
- Support shops and businesses that accept cash.  
- Refuse to cooperate with the abolition of cash.

Cash is not outdated.  
**Cash is freedom.**
