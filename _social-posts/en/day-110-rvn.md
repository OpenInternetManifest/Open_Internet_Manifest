### RVN: In the UFC only facts count – why is the West destroying itself? 🥊

Yesterday, **UFC Freedom 250** took place on the lawn of the White House — a historic spectacle in honour of America’s 250th anniversary of independence.

Pure meritocracy in action: Ilia Topuria, Justin Gaethje, Pereira, O’Malley and the rest. No quotas. No DEI. No “safe spaces”. Only those who train the hardest, are the strongest and fight the smartest, win. The scale and the cage don’t lie.

**In the UFC it works.**

Outside the UFC we see the exact opposite in the West:

- Schools that lower standards based on origin or gender.  
- Companies with diversity quotas instead of the best candidate.  
- Governments that ignore statistics on crime, performance or demography because they “are not allowed”.  
- A culture that actively demonises masculinity, Western values and white achievement history.

**The anti-Western self-hatred mechanism**

We are the only civilisation that systematically undermines its own foundations.

Imagine:  
- If all men disappeared tomorrow, how long would it take before the lights go out, the water stops flowing and the food supply chain collapses?  
- If men and women stood in direct opposition in a purely physical or existential confrontation — who would hold their ground?

Yet in the name of “equality”, “decolonisation” and “toxic masculinity” we portray exactly those builders of our civilisation as the problem.

Anti-white sentiment at universities, masculinity seen as suspicious, Western history equated with oppression.  
The result: falling birth rates, collapsing school performance among boys, a weakened society that calls itself “progressive” while slowly disintegrating.

**The big lie**

They tell us that diversity without demands is our strength.  
That meritocracy is “colonial” or “exclusive”.

But in the UFC — where real diversity (nationalities, ethnicities, fighting styles) actually exists — only the deserving win. And everyone accepts the outcome.

So why don’t we accept that in education, crime policy, immigration, technology or leadership?

**OIM way out**

Let’s take the UFC rules as a blueprint for society:

- Stop with quotas and positive discrimination.  
- Let performance, discipline and reality determine who moves forward.  
- Acknowledge that unequal outcomes often stem from unequal inputs (culture, behaviour, choices, biology).  
- Stop the anti-Western self-hatred that is hollowing us out from within.

A civilisation that dismantles its own foundations (masculinity, meritocracy, Western achievement) is committing cultural suicide.

**Question to you**

Do you want to live in a society that follows the rules of the UFC — hard, honest and based on reality?  
Or will you continue to participate in the anti-Western narrative that is slowly destroying us?

The cage never lies.  
It’s time the rest of the West stops lying too.
