### RVN: Cancel Culture & Deplatforming – the new book burning 🗣️🚫

Freedom of speech?  
Sure — as long as you say exactly what the narrative allows.

If you don’t, you get the modern version of book burnings:

- Fighters who make a joke about Michelle Obama → cancel attempt  
- Scientists who show statistics on crime or gender → fired or deplatformed  
- Citizens who use words like “omvolking” → parliamentary motions and police visits  
- Journalists, comedians, professors and ordinary people who step one foot outside the permitted thought lane → social death

**This is not coincidence. This is systemic.**

Big Tech, advertisers, universities, media and governments increasingly work together to silence unwanted voices.  
Not with boots or batons, but with algorithms, employment contracts, payment providers and “community guidelines”.

**The big lie**

They call it “fighting hate”, “protecting democracy” and “safety”.  
In reality they are protecting their own power and ideology.

The new fascism has no swastikas.  
It has a content moderation team, a DEI department and a subsidised fact-checker.

**This is exactly what Jacques Presser predicted**

The new fascism calls itself anti-fascism.  
It doesn’t suppress freedom in the name of the state, but in the name of “tolerance”, “inclusion” and “safety”.

**OIM way out**

We will not be silenced:

- Build parallel platforms and infrastructure that cannot be cancelled.  
- Refuse to play their language and cancel games.  
- Make everything verifiable (hashes, own nodes, open source).  
- Speak louder and clearer, especially when they try to shut you up.

Whoever is afraid of words is afraid of the truth.

**Question to you**

Have you ever been cancelled, deplatformed or silenced for saying something that wasn’t allowed?  
Or do you know someone who was?

How much longer will we accept that “antifascists” behave like the new censors?

The digital gag is on.  
Time to shake it off.
