### RVN: "All white men to the back" 🎤

At the Best Kept Secret Festival, singer Sophie Straat did something remarkable.

During her performance she called out:

**“All white men to the back. Queer, gay, women and people of colour can stay at the front.”**

Part of the audience cheered.  
Another part watched in disbelief.  
And rightly so.

This is not provocation or art.  
This is open **segregation based on race and gender** — but reversed.

**Imagine someone said the opposite.**  
“All dark men to the back, whites to the front.”  
Hell would break loose. Cancel culture, media storm, police, complaints.

But when it’s against white men?  
Then it’s suddenly “empowerment”, “provocation” or “art”.

**The big lie**

This is not resistance against discrimination.  
This is discrimination with a different colour.  
Anti-white sentiment and anti-male sentiment are currently the only forms of racism and sexism that are not only tolerated, but often actively applauded at festivals, universities and in the media.

The new fascism calls itself anti-fascism… and organises its own reversed apartheid in the meantime.

**OIM way out**

Real equality means no preferential treatment for anyone.  
No “white men to the back.”  
No “minorities to the front.”

Just one simple rule:  
Everyone equal before the law and on the festival grounds.

Anyone who uses race or gender to separate people is playing exactly the game they claim to fight.

**Question to you**

Would you still go to a festival where you are sent to the back based on skin colour or gender?  
And how long will we accept anti-white racism being sold as “progressive”?

This is not inclusion.  
This is reversed exclusion.
