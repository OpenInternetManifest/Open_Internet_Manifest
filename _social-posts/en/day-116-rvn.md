  ### RVN: The new fascism calls itself anti-fascism 🛡️
  
  In 1947, Dutch historian Jacques Presser wrote:
  
  > **“The new fascism will call itself anti-fascism.”**
  
  This week we saw that mechanism up close.
  
  **Wednesday** we saw a fighter who dared to say “Michelle Obama is a man” — and the left exploded, while they themselves have spent years denying what a woman is.  
  **Thursday** the ultimate hypocrisy: “My body my choice” for unborn babies, but “genocide!” for Gaza — while real massacres of Christians in Nigeria are ignored.  
  **Friday** cancel culture and deplatforming as the modern book burning.  
  **Saturday** institutional power: government, media, universities and Big Tech working together to silence unwanted voices.
  
  These are not isolated incidents.  
  This is a pattern.
  
  **The new fascism has no swastikas.**  
  It has no dictator in uniform.  
  It carries rainbow flags, DEI certificates, subsidy cheques and a moral superiority complex.
  
  It doesn’t come with boots, but with:
  - “Hate speech” laws and group defamation rules  
  - Cancel attempts and employment contracts  
  - Algorithms and fact-checkers  
  - Parliamentary motions that make ordinary words taboo
  
  It calls itself “tolerant”, “inclusive”, “antiracist” and “progressive” — while using exactly the same mechanisms it claims to fight: censorship, exclusion, demonisation and terror against dissenters.
  
  **This is Presser’s prediction in full glory.**
  
  Old fascism was openly authoritarian.  
  New fascism is smarter: it suffocates freedom in the name of freedom.  
  It suppresses debate in the name of democracy.  
  It hates in the name of love.
  
  **The big lie**
  
  They tell us “democracy is in danger” because of people like us.  
  In reality, democracy is in danger because of *them* — institutions that can no longer tolerate criticism and declare truth taboo.
  
  **OIM way out**
  
  We recognise the mechanism.  
  And we refuse to play along.
  
  - Build parallel structures that cannot be cancelled  
  - Make everything verifiable (hashes, own nodes, open source)  
  - Speak louder, not softer  
  - Withdraw from their systems where possible  
  - Call it by its name: this is the new fascism that calls itself anti-fascism
  
  A healthy society can handle the truth.  
  A sick society forbids the truth and calls it “civilisation”.
  
  **Question to you**
  
  Do you recognise the new fascism that calls itself anti-fascism?  
  And do you dare to name it out loud — even if they try to cancel you for it?
  
  Jacques Presser was right in 1947.  
  Let’s make sure he doesn’t turn out to be right in 2026.
  
  History doesn’t repeat itself.  
  But it does rhyme.

### RVN: The new fascism calls itself anti-fascism 🛡️

In 1947, Dutch historian Jacques Presser wrote:

> **“The new fascism will call itself anti-fascism.”**

This week we saw that mechanism up close.

**Wednesday** we saw a fighter who dared to say “Michelle Obama is a man” — and the left exploded, while they themselves have spent years denying what a woman is.  
**Thursday** the ultimate hypocrisy: “My body my choice” for unborn babies, but “genocide!” for Gaza — while real massacres of Christians in Nigeria are ignored.  
**Friday** cancel culture and deplatforming as the modern book burning.  
**Saturday** institutional power: government, media, universities and Big Tech working together to silence unwanted voices.

These are not isolated incidents.  
This is a pattern.

**The new fascism has no swastikas.**  
It has no dictator in uniform.  
It carries rainbow flags, DEI certificates, subsidy cheques and a moral superiority complex.

It doesn’t come with boots, but with:
- “Hate speech” laws and group defamation rules  
- Cancel attempts and employment contracts  
- Algorithms and fact-checkers  
- Parliamentary motions that make ordinary words taboo

It calls itself “tolerant”, “inclusive”, “antiracist” and “progressive” — while using exactly the same mechanisms it claims to fight: censorship, exclusion, demonisation and terror against dissenters.

**This is Presser’s prediction in full glory.**

Old fascism was openly authoritarian.  
New fascism is smarter: it suffocates freedom in the name of freedom.  
It suppresses debate in the name of democracy.  
It hates in the name of love.

**The big lie**

They tell us “democracy is in danger” because of people like us.  
In reality, democracy is in danger because of *them* — institutions that can no longer tolerate criticism and declare truth taboo.

**OIM way out**

We recognise the mechanism.  
And we refuse to play along.

- Build parallel structures that cannot be cancelled  
- Make everything verifiable (hashes, own nodes, open source)  
- Speak louder, not softer  
- Withdraw from their systems where possible  
- Call it by its name: this is the new fascism that calls itself anti-fascism

A healthy society can handle the truth.  
A sick society forbids the truth and calls it “civilisation”.

**Question to you**

Do you recognise the new fascism that calls itself anti-fascism?  
And do you dare to name it out loud — even if they try to cancel you for it?

Jacques Presser was right in 1947.  
Let’s make sure he doesn’t turn out to be right in 2026.

History doesn’t repeat itself.  
But it does rhyme.
