**RVN: “This was all staged for…” – The Trap of THE Truth** 🌀

**Narrative:**  
“All the fires were staged. This is clearly preparation for the smart city and 15-minute cities. Wake up!”

**Reality:**

We see this pattern more and more: as soon as something happens (fires, floods, attacks, price spikes), someone immediately claims **THE truth**.  
One simple, all-encompassing explanation. Often linked to a big agenda (WEF, smart cities, deep state, etc.).

This sounds like being awake, but it is a **subtle trap**.

**Never just one crisis, always multiple layers**

As Rahm Emanuel once said: **“Never waste a good crisis.”**

Crises are almost always used to serve multiple goals at the same time.

**Example: Fires on military grounds**
- Acceleration of **digitization and control** (smart city agenda)
- Argument for **more EU defense cooperation** (“we need Europe for a strong army – alone is just alone”)
- Strengthening the **climate narrative** (“see, extreme weather due to climate change”)
- Distraction from other sensitive issues
- Budget increases for defense and security

**The danger of claiming THE truth**
By immediately saying “this is all for the 15-minute city”:
- You reduce complex reality to one narrative
- You immediately create enemies (“if you don’t believe this, you’re blind!”)
- You feed exactly the **divide & conquer** mechanism that systems love to use

**OIM way out**

Stop claiming **THE** truth.  
Instead, look for **layers** and build outside of them.

- What interests are really at play?
- Which mechanisms do we see recurring?
- And most importantly: what are you going to build yourself instead of just reacting?

**Question to you**  
Do you recognize the pattern that every event is immediately linked to one big agenda?  
Do you often claim “this is it”, or are you willing to allow for multiple layers?

Let’s stop feeding polarization and start building real alternatives.
