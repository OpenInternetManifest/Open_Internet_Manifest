Many Americans admit:
“I thought Europeans were boring and arrogant. This is next level.”

**The big lie is cracking on both sides.**

Europe has been fed anti-American sentiment for years.  
America has been given a picture of Europeans as spoiled, decadent and anti-freedom.

And now, at the World Cup, ordinary people on both sides are seeing the reality:  
Europeans can still party hard, stand behind their country and turn an entire stadium upside down.

**This is no coincidence.**

The narrative of division between Western peoples is actively maintained — especially by a leadership that benefits from a weak, divided West.

**OIM way out**

The best way to defeat narratives is direct encounter.

People who meet each other in real life often feel the natural connection immediately.  
Not through media, not through politics, but through shared passion (football, partying, freedom).

That is exactly why we build parallel structures:  
To let people connect directly again, without the toxic filter of the elite.

**Question to you**

Do you feel that natural bond between Western peoples when you see videos of Oranje fans and Americans singing “Links, Rechts!” together?

Or do you think the elite fears exactly this kind of moment?

Perhaps that is the biggest threat to their power:  
A united West without their interference.
