For a Free, Open and Sovereign Internet

Create a Nostr account

Nostr is a decentralized, open protocol for social media. It is censorship-resistant, has no central company, and gives you full control over your data and identity.

Time: 5–10 minutes
Cost: €0

Step 1 – Choose a Nostr client

Popular and easy clients:

  • Amethyst (Android) – recommended for beginners
  • Damus (iOS)
  • Coracle or Iris (Web)
  • Primal (Web + App)

Step 2 – Create your account

  1. Open your chosen client (e.g. Amethyst)
  2. Choose Create new account
  3. Enter a username (this is your display name)
  4. Write a short bio (optional)
  5. Important: Save your private key (nsec…) somewhere safe!
    This is your password — if you lose it, you lose your account.

Tip: Many clients offer to save the key as a QR code or text file. Do this immediately.

Step 3 – Get your public key (npub)

Your public key (starts with npub...) is your address on Nostr.
Share this one with others — never share your private key.

Step 4 – Follow people & hashtags

  • Search for people you know
  • Follow popular hashtags: #nostr, #pleb, #bitcoin, #opensource, #privacy
  • Use nostr.directory or nostr.band to discover users

Step 5 – Start posting

Write your first note:
“Hello Nostr! I just created my account and left [Twitter/Instagram] behind. Who should I follow?”

You now have a truly decentralized social identity.
