This week in teasers:  
🔹 A Jewish woman in Amsterdam who hesitates for a moment before daring to say who she is 😔  
🔹 An Iranian woman who can no longer reach her family because the internet is blacked out 🌑  
🔹 Creators being banned for showing what others hide 🚫  
🔹 Blackouts silencing voices, but Starlink providing a lifeline 🛰️  

The narrative wants control.  
Reality wants freedom 🕊️

In the Bible, Israel is called the promised land, and the Jewish people the chosen people.  
Not as superiority, but as responsibility: a light unto the nations, an example of freedom and covenant with God 🌟

But every promise can be abused.  
What if identity is used for power? ⚠️  
What if polarization is deliberately fueled to turn peoples against each other? 🔥

The Open Internet Manifest stands for an internet where identity is verifiable, where freedom is not selective, and where boundaries are voluntary and distributed — not centrally imposed 🚀

No gatekeepers deciding who may speak 🚫  
No black boxes watching along 👀  
No manipulation of narratives 🧠

This is not a dream from December 2025.  
This is a call for right now. 🙏
