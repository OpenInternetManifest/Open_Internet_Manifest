# Thesis 29 – The open internet is not an utopia, it is a daily choice

Every time you choose an open alternative instead of convenience, you push the gate open one centimeter further.

Every time you leave a closed platform, their grip becomes a little weaker.

Freedom is not a destination — it is a habit you can pick up again today.
