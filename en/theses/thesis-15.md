# Thesis 15 – Social networks without a central owner

[**Mastodon**](/Open_Internet_Manifest/en/concepts/mastodon), [**Pixelfed**](/Open_Internet_Manifest/en/concepts/pixelfed), [**Lemmy**](/Open_Internet_Manifest/en/concepts/lemmy), [**Friendica**](/Open_Internet_Manifest/en/concepts/friendica), [**Hubzilla**](/Open_Internet_Manifest/en/concepts/hubzilla): you choose your own server (instance) or run it yourself.

You can always move with all your followers, posts, and media — no more hostage-taking.

It already works today for millions of people. The only thing still missing is you and me.
