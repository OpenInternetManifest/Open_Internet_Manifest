# Security

The excuse gatekeepers use to justify total control.

They claim to protect you from viruses, hackers, and “dangerous content,” while the biggest threat to your privacy and freedom is themselves.

Real security comes from open standards, end-to-end encryption, and decentralization — not from one company that sees everything.
