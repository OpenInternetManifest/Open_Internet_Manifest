# Peer-to-peer payments

Direct payments between individuals without intermediaries like banks, PayPal, or Apple Pay.

Examples: Bitcoin/Lightning, Monero, open source wallets, Nostr zaps.

Enables micro-payments, international transfers, and donations without commission or censorship.
