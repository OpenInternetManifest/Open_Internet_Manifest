# Surveillance

The systematic and permanent monitoring of citizens by companies or governments, often without consent or transparency.

On the internet, this translates into cameras in your home (smart home), microphones in your phone, and trackers on every website.

Offline, this would be a scandal. Online, it’s called “service”.
