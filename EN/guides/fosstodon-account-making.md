# Guide – Create an account on Fosstodon (tech and open-source community)

Fosstodon is a Mastodon instance focused on open-source, tech, privacy, and free software — perfect for developers, makers, and privacy-conscious people.

**Time:** 10–15 minutes  
**Cost:** €0

### Step 1 – Go to Fosstodon
Go directly to <a href="https://fosstodon.org" target="_blank">fosstodon.org</a>

### Step 2 – Create an account
<details>
<summary>Step-by-step</summary>

1. Click “Join” or “Create account”
2. Fill in a username (e.g. @yourname)
3. Fill in email and password
4. Accept the rules (fosstodon is friendly but moderated)
5. Verify your email → done!
</details>

### Step 3 – Set up your profile
<details>
<summary>Make it recognizable</summary>

- Use a profile picture that fits you (tech-related or not)
- Bio: briefly tell who you are and what interests you (open-source, privacy, etc.)
- Add your old X/Twitter handle in your bio if you want
- Pin a post: “Just started on Fosstodon — hello open-source world!”
</details>

### Step 4 – Follow people
<details>
<summary>Tips for the tech community</summary>

- Follow hashtags like #FOSS, #Linux, #Privacy, #OpenSource
- Search for project names or people you know
- Use <a href="https://fedifinder.glitch.me" target="_blank">Fedifinder</a> to find your old followers on Mastodon
</details>

### Step 5 – Make your first post
Post something about open-source, privacy, or why you switched.

You are now part of a community that values quality over quantity.
