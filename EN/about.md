# Open Internet Manifesto

In the real world, you always know how you got somewhere: on foot, by bike, via a beautiful route, or in pouring rain.  
On the current internet, you are teleported to a few closed amusement parks without knowing who controls the gates.

This manifesto wants to give back an internet with real roads, squares, and paths ‚Äî an internet where you choose the route yourself.

A living manifesto for a free, open, and decentralized internet ‚Äî built and owned by the community.
