# Create an account on Fosstodon

Fosstodon is a Mastodon instance with a strong focus on open-source, technology, privacy, and free software — perfect for developers, makers, and privacy-conscious people.

**Time:** 10–15 minutes  
**Cost:** €0

### Step 1 – Go to Fosstodon

Go directly to [fosstodon.org](https://fosstodon.org)

### Step 2 – Create an account

1. Click **Join** or **Create account**
2. Choose a username (e.g. `@yourname`)
3. Enter your email address and a strong password
4. Accept the server rules (Fosstodon is friendly but actively moderated)
5. Verify your email → done!

### Step 3 – Set up your profile

- Choose a recognizable profile picture (tech-related is nice but not required)
- Write a short bio (who you are and what interests you: open-source, privacy, Linux…)
- Optionally add your old X/Twitter handle in your bio
- Pin a welcome post: “Just started on Fosstodon — hello open-source world!”

### Step 4 – Follow people

- Follow hashtags like #FOSS, #OpenSource, #Linux, #Privacy, #SelfHosting
- Search for names of projects or people you know
- Use [Fedifinder](https://fedifinder.glitch.me) to find your old Twitter/X followers

### Step 5 – Make your first post

Post something about open-source, privacy, a tool you use, or why you switched.

You are now part of a community that values quality over quantity.
