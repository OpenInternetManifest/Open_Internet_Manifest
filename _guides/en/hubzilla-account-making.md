# Create your first Hubzilla account

Hubzilla is a powerful decentralized platform that combines social networking, cloud storage, blogs, and **nomadic identity**. Your data and identity travel with you, wherever you go.

**Time:** 20–30 minutes  
**Cost:** €0

### Step 1 – Choose a hub

Good starters for beginners:
- [hubzilla.nl](https://hubzilla.nl) – Dutch hub, beginner-friendly
- [hub.libranet.de](https://hub.libranet.de) – German, privacy-focused and active
- [zotum.net](https://zotum.net) – stable general hub

Or browse more hubs at [hubzilla.org/hub-directory](https://hubzilla.org/hub-directory).

### Step 2 – Create an account

1. Go to your chosen hub’s website
2. Click **Register** or **Create account**
3. Enter a username, email address and strong password
4. Complete registration (sometimes moderated)
5. Verify your email → done!

### Step 3 – Set up your profile and identity

- Choose a recognizable profile picture and write a clear bio
- Create additional **channels** if you want multiple identities
- Enable nomadic identity (you can easily move to another hub later without losing data)
- Connect with other networks (Mastodon, Friendica, Diaspora, etc.)

### Step 4 – Make connections

- Search and add contacts within the Zot network
- Connect with the wider fediverse (Mastodon, Friendica)
- Import contacts from other systems

### Step 5 – Make your first post

Write a post, share a photo or file — Hubzilla automatically distributes it across your connected networks.

You now have a full, portable and independent digital identity.
