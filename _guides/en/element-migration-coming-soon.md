# Replace WhatsApp with Element (Matrix)

Element is end-to-end encrypted, federated, and you are not dependent on Meta.

**Time:** 20–40 minutes **Cost:** €0

### Step 1 – Download Element

**On phone and computer:**

- Android: [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=im.vector.app) or [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/packages/im.vector.app/) (recommended)
- iOS: [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/element-messenger/id1083446067)
- Desktop: [element.io/get-started](https://element.io/get-started) (Windows, Mac, Linux)
- Web: [app.element.io](https://app.element.io)

### Step 2 – Create an account

**You don’t need to give a phone number:**

1. Open Element
2. Choose **Create Account**
3. Choose a homeserver:
   - matrix.org (largest, easiest)
   - or a more privacy-friendly one like chat.mozilla.org
4. Choose a username and strong password
5. Save your recovery key (very important!)

### Step 3 – Add your first contacts

Ask friends and family for their Matrix ID (@name:matrix.org).

You can also search by email or phone number if they have enabled it.

**Tip:** Start with 3–5 people you trust.

### Step 4 – Move groups and chats

1. Create a new Room in Element
2. Invite people via their Matrix ID
3. Share the room link in your WhatsApp group
4. Optionally: use a bridge bot (temporary)

### Step 5 – Phase out or leave WhatsApp

- Put in your WhatsApp status: “I’m now on Element: @yourname:matrix.org”
- Only delete WhatsApp when you feel comfortable on Element

**Result:** Your messages are end-to-end encrypted, you can always switch servers, and your data is truly yours.
