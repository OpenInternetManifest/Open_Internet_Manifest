# Messaging apps without Big Tech

Big Tech messaging apps (WhatsApp, iMessage, Telegram, Facebook Messenger) are convenient, but they come with surveillance, central control and lock-in. Here are better alternatives.

**Recommended apps (2026)**

| App          | Encryption       | Decentralized | Best for                          | Platforms                  | Difficulty |
|--------------|------------------|---------------|-----------------------------------|----------------------------|------------|
| **Element** (Matrix) | E2EE             | Yes           | Groups, communities, daily use    | Android, iOS, Desktop, Web | Easy       |
| **Signal**   | E2EE             | No            | Maximum security, simple          | Android, iOS, Desktop      | Very Easy  |
| **Session**  | E2EE             | Yes           | Strong anonymity                  | Android, iOS, Desktop      | Easy       |
| **Threema**  | E2EE             | No            | Paid, no phone number required    | Android, iOS, Desktop      | Easy       |
| **Simplex**  | E2EE             | Yes           | No user IDs, maximum privacy      | Android, iOS, Desktop      | Medium     |

### Recommended starting point: Element (Matrix)

**Why Element?**
- Truly decentralized and federated
- End-to-end encrypted by default
- Large communities and bridges to other networks
- You can self-host or use public servers

**Quick start:**
1. Download Element from [element.io](https://element.io) or app stores
2. Create account on a server (e.g. `matrix.org`, `chat.mozilla.org` or a privacy-focused one)
3. Save your recovery key!
4. Add contacts via their Matrix ID (`@username:server.com`)
5. Start chatting or join rooms

**Pro tip:** Use bridges to stay in touch with people still on WhatsApp (temporary solution).

### Other strong options

- **Signal** → Best security + usability balance (but centralized)
- **Session** → No phone number, onion routing
- **Simplex** → No persistent user IDs at all

**Migration tip:**  
Put in your old WhatsApp/Telegram status: “I’m now on Element: @yourname:matrix.org” and share your new contact.

You no longer need to give your data to Big Tech to stay in touch with friends and family.
