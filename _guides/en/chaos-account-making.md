# Create an account on Chaos.social

Chaos.social is a Mastodon instance focused on privacy, freedom, and an active, friendly community — ideal for people who want a lively but non-commercial environment.

**Time:** 10–15 minutes  
**Cost:** €0

### Step 1 – Go to Chaos.social

Go directly to [chaos.social](https://chaos.social)

### Step 2 – Create an account

1. Click **Registrieren** or **Sign up**
2. Fill in a username (e.g. `@yourname`)
3. Fill in your email address and a strong password
4. Accept the house rules (Chaos.social is actively moderated but free)
5. Verify your email → done!

### Step 3 – Set up your profile

- Choose a recognizable profile picture
- Write a short bio (who you are and what interests you: privacy, tech, freedom…)
- Optionally add your old X/Twitter handle in your bio
- Pin a welcome post: “Just started on Chaos.social — hello free fediverse!”

### Step 4 – Follow people

- Follow hashtags like #ChaosSocial, #Privacy, #Fediverse, #FOSS
- Search for names or handles of people you know
- Use [Fedifinder](https://fedifinder.glitch.me) to find your old Twitter/X followers

### Step 5 – Make your first post

Post something about privacy, freedom, or why you switched.

You are now part of an active, free and non-commercial community.
