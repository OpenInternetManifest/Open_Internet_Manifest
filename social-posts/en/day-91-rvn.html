RVN: “Palestine 3000 years of history vs Israel 60 years of lies” 🌀

Narrative:
Palestine has 3000 years of history. Israel is only 60 years old and built on lies and colonialism.

Reality:

This is one of the most persistent historical manipulations of our time. Let’s unfold the facts calmly.

The Origin of the Name “Palestine”

The name “Palestine” comes from the Philistines (Peleshet), a non-Semitic seafaring people who settled the coastal area of Canaan around 1200 BC. The Greeks later called the region “Palaistine”.

The crucial political name change came after the Bar Kokhba Revolt (132–136 AD). Emperor Hadrian deliberately renamed the province of Judea to Syria Palaestina as punishment and an attempt to erase Jewish identity and connection to the land.

Jews remained the majority or a very large minority in the area for centuries afterward.

Demographic Reality Then and Now

Until the 7th century, the region was largely Jewish and Christian. Then came the Islamic conquest.

Current demographics (2026):

  • Israel: ±73% Jewish, ±21% Arab/Muslim
  • West Bank: ±99% Arab/Muslim
  • Gaza: ±99.5% Arab/Muslim
  • Jordan: ±95% Arab/Muslim (before 1948 largely mixed Jewish-Arab)
  • Lebanon: ±60% Muslim (was Christian majority before 1975)
  • Syria: ±90% Arab/Muslim

What stands out: in the neighboring countries, the Jewish percentage dropped to almost zero after 1948 (often through expulsion and pogroms). In Israel, a substantial Arab minority remained (21%).

This shows that the story of “Israel stole the land from the Palestinians” is historically dishonest. The area has always had a mix of populations, but Jews were largely driven out of the surrounding Arab countries.

Modern Islamic Imperialism

While Israel is constantly accused of “colonialism”, people ignore the much larger and longer Arab-Islamic imperialism: conquests from the 7th century onward, including the subjugation of Christian and Jewish populations under dhimmi status.

OIM Way Out

History is not a shopping cart where you pick what fits your narrative.
A people may exist. A culture may be protected. A country may defend its own identity.

Let’s stop rewriting history because reality doesn’t fit the desired story.

Question to you

Do you seriously believe that “Palestine 3000 years of history” is an honest historical claim?
Or is this mainly a modern political trick to undermine Jewish (and Western) self-determination?

Let’s acknowledge facts.
Let’s stop using history as a weapon.
